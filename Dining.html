Dining and drinks



Freestyle Pool Bar

Freestyle Pool Bar is an enjoyable spot for a refreshing break after a swim.

Delight in delectable light snacks and expertly concocted cocktails, or be

entertained with live mixology at the swim-up bar.



Hua Yuan



Hua Yuan offers Shanghainese cuisine revisited in a modern way. The casual yet upscale



restaurant serves authentic Shanghainese favorites that are complementary, daring



and creatively presented. Among the standout dishes are dumplings including Xiao Long Bao,



Hanging Roasted Duck, Red Braised Pork, and seafood such as Hairy Crab and Crayfish. For



exclusive affairs, the restaurant has three private dining rooms that can fit from 10



to as much as 12 people.




Kusina Sea Kitchens

Kusina is a vibrant and colorful all-day interactive buffet outlet serving

breakfast, lunch and dinner daily. Located on the third floor of the hotel,

the restaurant features a wholesome breakfast with Asian and Western options

that evolves to a more Filipino offering during lunch and dinner with a

focus on fresh seafood and both local and international favorites.



Madison Lounge & Bar



Showcasing the pulsating urban vibe of New York City, Madison captures the



ambiance of a modern café and lounge. Madison Lounge + Bar is a sophisticated place



that transitions to suit today’s multi-faceted lifestyle - an airy café in the morning,



offering an artisanal coffee selection and Grab & Go choices for people on-the-go;



and a sophisticated bar in the evening, ideal for pre or after dinner cocktails.




Port

Paying homage to the Port of Manila, the largest and historically significant

shipping gateway to the Philippines, Port Bar makes a perfect place to

unwind for casual drink. Guests can enjoy the finest range of local,

international and craft beers as well as amber spirits including whiskeys,

cognacs and dark rums from around the world
